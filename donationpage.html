Our services

Get a brief idea about our services and give us a little support to protect these turtles (donations or you can purchase our products).

Guarding the hatcheries to keep the eggs safe.

Prevent eggs from being broken in order to minimize a hen learning to eat an egg and developing egg eating habits. Free choice oyster shells will help strengthen the egg shells. Keep rats, cats, predators and snakes away from the hen house. They often will eat the feed and eggs and contaminate the nesting boxes, feed and other eggs.

Helping injured turtles

when we found a injured turtle first we check the details of the turtle and then check for the injury parts, then we safely put the turtle in to the perticular tank. after he healing we get him back to the ocean .

Releasing baby turtles to the sea and cleaning the tanks

We collect the baby turtles to a separate tank and then we are raising them untill theire body get strong.and then we relase them in to the sea with right conditiond
